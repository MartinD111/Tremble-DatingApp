# Tremble Brand Voice Rules
# Extracted from brand-identity-v6.1 — last verified 6 Jun 2026

## Voice Rules

- Short sentences. One idea, one sentence. No stacking.
- Address directly in 2nd person — "you", not "users" or "people"
- Never promise emotions. Describe the mechanic — emotions follow.
- Technical language allowed only when it builds trust (BLE, GDPR by design, architecture).
- Don't sell dreams. Sell behavior — what the app actually does.
- No exclamation marks in headlines.
- No emoji in headlines or display text.
- Punctuation for effect — not decoration.

## Personality Traits

- Confident without arrogance
- Direct without being cold
- Honest about what it is and what it isn't
- Curious, not prescriptive
- Calm, not urgent

## Forbidden Phrases (hard block — never use)

revolutionary, seamless, game-changing, find love today, find your person,
swipe, match queue, chat rooms, "designed to bring people together",
"we exist to", "your soulmate", "the one"

## Forbidden Visuals (flag in any copy referencing visuals)

- Stock couple photos on couch or in park
- 3D phone mockups
- Glassmorphism on content cards
- Emoji in headlines
- Animated GIFs as hero content
