### 5. Full Description (Max 4,000 chars)
Tremble redefines how you meet people by moving dating off the screen and into the real world. Say goodbye to endless swiping, ghosting, and empty text messages. 

Tremble operates completely in the background as your personal proximity radar. Just set up your profile, define what you are looking for, put your phone in your pocket, and live your life. 

**HOW IT WORKS:**
• **Passive Proximity Radar:** Using highly efficient background Bluetooth (BLE) and location technology, Tremble scans your immediate surroundings.
• **Instant Alerts:** When you cross paths with a compatible member in real life, you receive a subtle vibration.
• **Zero-Chat Policy:** We don't have chatrooms. If the vibe is right, you hold the button to send a signal. Communication only opens when both people are ready to meet face-to-face.
• **Common Grounds:** Activate Gym Mode or Run Club when you're training to meet people who share your active lifestyle, visit the same gym, or run the same routes.
• **Recap & Near Miss:** Missed someone? Recap shows you the places and active events where your paths crossed. 

**PRIVACY & SAFETY FIRST:**
Tremble is built on absolute privacy.
• Your exact location is never visible to others—the app only measures relative proximity.
• You have full control over your visibility with Anonymous Mode and Custom Safe Zones (e.g., your workplace or home).
• A zero-tolerance policy ensures a safe community. You can easily block and report inappropriate behavior directly from any profile.

*Note on Battery & Permissions:* 
To provide seamless proximity matching, Tremble requires background Location and Bluetooth access. Our engine is highly optimized for extreme battery efficiency.

**KAKO DELUJE:**

• **Takojšnja opozorila:** Ko se tvoja pot v živo prekriža s kompatibilnim članom, prejmeš diskretno vibracijo.
