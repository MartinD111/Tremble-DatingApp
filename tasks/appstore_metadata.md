### 5. Full Description (Max 4,000 chars)
Tremble runs in the background while you live your life.

No swiping. No chat. No algorithm deciding who you should meet.

When someone compatible is physically nearby, Tremble notices.
You get a signal. One button. Send a wave or don't. If they wave
back, you have 30 minutes to find each other in the real world.
That's the entire app.

HOW IT WORKS:
Tremble uses Bluetooth and GPS to detect real physical proximity.
Your exact location is never stored. Not on our servers. Not anywhere.
We store only a proximity signal — and only while it's active.

WAVE:
One signal. No noise. If both people wave, a 30-minute window opens.
Find each other or don't. The clock is real. So is the person.

RUN CLUB:
Running the same route as someone compatible? Tremble notices mid-run.
After, you have 10 minutes to act. Then the recap is gone.

GYM MODE:
Training at the same gym? Tremble stays silent during your workout.
When you're done, it shows you who was there.

PRIVACY:
Your location is never stored or transmitted. This is architecture,
not policy. Bluetooth proximity is computed in memory and discarded.
Safe Zones block detection near your home or workplace.

Tremble is available to users 18 and over.
Bluetooth and background location access required for proximity detection.
